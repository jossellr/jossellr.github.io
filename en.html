Who I am

My name is Jossé Luis, I'm 26 years old and I live in Jaén. I graduated in Computer Science Engineering at the University of Jaén in 2018 (honorary distinction in my final project and an average mark of 7.93) and at the beginning of 2020 I finished my Master's Degree in Computer Science Engineering at the same university (honorary distinction in my final project and an average mark of 9.04).

Within my degree I specialized in Information Technology: web technology, networks, databases... I also got a mention in Graphics, allowing me to know in depth animation techniques, lighting with shaders and the expansion with WebGL of the web environment.

In parallel to my university life, I have been working for a total period of two years on research assignments.
